Help and FAQ
+What does the "I'm Feeling Adventurous" button do?

It will randomly choose a category for you and then randomly choose an establishment for you. For the true explorers!

+What if I don't like any of the choices chosen for me?

Unfortunately, we utilize the Google Maps API to help search for places for you based on your current location or the location you specified. You can increase the distance radius to include more places, but it will also allow results much farther from you. This is where you'll have to explore by going to new areas.

+What if I'm feeling something else other than the categories listed?

Unforunately, we have not been able to incorporate every single category of establishments yet. We intend to expand the filtering system in the future.
