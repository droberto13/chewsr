Chewsr is an app that helps a user explore the many different type of food places nearby when you get tired of the same, usual, common place. Chewsr has a wide spectrum of places of choose from and all at very close local range. Directions are very simple. To adjust the range of radius, go to "Settings". Then, you can either find food based on the type of food you are craving by tapping "Search Current Location", or you can search any category by tapping "I'm Feeling Adventurous". Then, the app randomizes your choices within the range you are looking for. Now go and have fun trying out the many different typees of food your community has to offer!
